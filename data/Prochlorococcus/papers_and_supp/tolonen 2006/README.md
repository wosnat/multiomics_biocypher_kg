**Citation:** Tolonen AC, Aach J, Lindell D, Johnson ZI, Rector T, Steen R, Church GM, Chisholm SW (2006). Global gene expression of *Prochlorococcus* ecotypes in response to changes in nitrogen availability. *Molecular Systems Biology* 2:53.
**DOI:** 10.1038/msb4100087
**Organism(s):** *Prochlorococcus* MED4 and MIT9313
**Topic:** Custom Affymetrix MD4-9313 microarray (Goldenspike DE) of both ecotypes during abrupt N deprivation (0/3/6/12/24/48h timecourse) and during steady-state growth on alternative N sources (cyanate + urea for MED4; nitrite + urea for MIT9313). Accompanied by K-means clusters (K=9 for MED4, K=7 for MIT9313) derived from the time course.

## Notes

- Gene IDs: `PMM####` (MED4) and `PMT####` (MIT9313) — both are `old_locus_tag` style and bridge via the v2 mapping. MIT9313 uses the `MIT9313_resources` paperconfig's GenBank bridge.
- Significance coding: `pvalue_asterisk_in_logfc: true`, `pvalue_threshold: 0.01`. Double asterisk (`**`) means q<0.01; single asterisk (`*`) means 0.01<q<0.05 — the parser treats either as significant at its configured threshold.
- Cluster methods: `time_course` / K-means with K=9 (MED4) and K=7 (MIT9313). These are valid soft-clustering assignments (not flag columns), so the `gene_clusters` type is correct — do not move to `derived_metrics_table`.
- The `medExpression.1.csv` and `mitExpression.1.csv` tables each drive 8 statistical analyses (6 timepoints + 2 alt-N steady-state conditions) within one supplementary-materials entry.
