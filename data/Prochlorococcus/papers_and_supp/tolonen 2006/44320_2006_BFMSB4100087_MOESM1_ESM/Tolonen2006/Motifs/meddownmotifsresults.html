Tolonen et al. 2006: Upstream motifs in MED4 repressed K-means clusters 7 and 8

As a motif matching the NtcA binding site was detected in up-regulated MED4 clusters 1 and 2, we hypothesized that AlignACE should be able to detect a similar motif if it is present upstream of repressed genes. The lack of any motif resembling the NtcA binding site suggests that NtcA does not play a major role in directly repressing these genes during N starvation.

AlignACE generated 17 motifs with columns=8 and 12 motifs with columns=10.

The best MAP score for columns=8 or 10 was 8.69716 (motif 1). The best specificity score for columns=8 was 4.36e-8 (motif 8), and for columns=10 was 1.97e-9 (motif 6).

CompareACE scores using the Su et al. (2005) NtcA motif as input were all poor (maximum 0.338805 for motif 5, columns=8).
