# Thompson 2016

**Citation:** Thompson LR, Zeng Q, Chisholm SW (2016). Gene Expression Patterns during Light and Dark Infection of *Prochlorococcus* by Cyanophage. *PLOS ONE* 11(10):e0165375.
**DOI:** 10.1371/journal.pone.0165375
**Organism(s):** *Prochlorococcus* MED4 (host); cyanophage P-HM2 (infection vector)
**Topic:** RNA-seq (DESeq2) time course of *Prochlorococcus* MED4 infected with cyanophage P-HM2 (MOI=1) vs uninfected spent-medium controls, sampled at 0.5/1.5/2.5/4.5/8.5h post-inoculation in both continuous light (90 µE m-2 s-1) and constant darkness. Two contrast families: (a) dark vs light within each infection state; (b) phage lysate vs spent medium within each light condition. Supplied tables are pre-filtered to significant DEGs only.

## Notes

- Gene IDs use native MED4 NCBI `locus_tag` format (`PMM####`) — no bridge file required; very high match rate expected.
- `pvalue_threshold: 0.2` and `logfc_threshold: 1.5` (dark-vs-light) or `0.4` (lysate-vs-spent). These are author-chosen; note the unusually permissive adjusted-p threshold.
- All analyses have `prefiltered: true`; the KG treats every row as significant (both `significant_up` and `significant_down` derivable from sign of log2FC).
- No `adjusted_p_value_col` is set — expression edges will have `adjusted_p_value` null.
- Dark-vs-light t=0.5h for uninfected is absent (authors compared infected and uninfected cultures differently at baseline); this is intentional, not a missing file.
