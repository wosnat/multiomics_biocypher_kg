# Weissberg 2025

**Citation:** Weissberg O, Aharonovich D, Sher D (2025). Transcriptomic and Proteomic Analysis Reveals Nitrogen Recycling as a Core Mechanism for *Prochlorococcus* Prolonged Survival. bioRxiv preprint.
**DOI:** 10.1101/2025.11.24.690089
**Organism(s):** *Prochlorococcus* MED4 and *Alteromonas macleodii* HOT1A3
**Topic:** Multi-omics (RNA-seq + proteomics via DESeq2) of a 90-day co-culture vs axenic cultures in PRO99-lowN medium (continuous light, 24 °C). Probes how HOT1A3 supplies recycled NH4+ to MED4 during long-term N starvation and extends MED4 survival. Time points: exponential (day 7 / day 11), onset of starvation (day 14 / day 18), deep starvation (day 31), long-term survival (days 60, 89, and a pooled "days 60+89"). Two contrast families per organism × omics: (a) late day vs exponential day within one growth condition; (b) coculture vs axenic at exponential day 11.
